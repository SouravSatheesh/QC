Drishti Quiz

The quiz club organises multiple quizzes for Drishti which is the technical fest of the college every year.Again Drishti is one of the most important events in the kerala quizzing calendar and quizzers from all over Kerala comes down to our college to attend the quiz hosted by the top QMs of the country and also to witness the cultural extravaganza
