The CET Quiz Club

This is the offical website of the CET quiz club which is one of the most prestigious and active college quiz club in Kerala.

We organise various college and open events throughout the year which are hosted and attended by the top quizzers in the country

We meet informally every week and the goal of the club is promote and spread love for this fun activity of quizzing
